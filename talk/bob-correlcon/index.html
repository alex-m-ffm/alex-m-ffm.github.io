Talking to data scientists about bees

Video

Abstract

For CorrelCon, the annual meetup of CorrelAid, my project teammates Diren Senger, Alexander Goncharskiy and I presented our work in the Bee Observer project to fellow CorrelAiders and potential future partner NPOs.

Date
Nov 7, 2020
13:00 — 15:00
Event
CorrelCon 2020
Location
remote

During our first talk on CorrelCon 2020, my project teammates Diren Senger (University of Bremen), Alexander Goncharskiy and I (both Correlaid x Rhein-Main) presented our work in the Bee Observer project, along with the general topic of bees and what Citizen Science means. Watch the recording of the talk to get a flavour.
